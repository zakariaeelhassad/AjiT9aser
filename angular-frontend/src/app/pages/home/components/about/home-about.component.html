About Valhalla

What is
Valhalla?

Valhalla (Moroccan darija for "Come on, compete!") is a fantasy football game built around real Premier League matches. Each week you assemble a squad, captain your best player, and rack up points based on their real-world performances.

You start with a 100.0 budget to sign exactly 15 players spanning goalkeepers, defenders, midfielders, and forwards. Every goal, assist, and card feeds into your score.

Compete on the global leaderboard, or challenge friends. Strategy and form-spotting will determine who lifts the trophy.
